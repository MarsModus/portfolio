ABOUT ME

Former Geomatics & Surveying Engineer, that has turned to web development and never looked back. I love every aspect of it and have never been as passionate about anything.

Taking every opportunity I had to work with other devolopers right from the beginning, I mainly worked with CMS as a Content Admnistrator, using Wordpress and Joomla. Eventually, I applied what I learned and created my first websites from scratch.

Three years have passed since I started this wonderful journey. I have completed online courses and bootcamps, offline seminars for networking and I have been through tutorial hell. I would love to join a team of developers and work on bigger projects in a higher paced enviroment.

Web development is ever evolving, with innovations around every corner, therefore a web developer is always learning and adapting. Complementing each skill with the right tools, striving to achieve the best results, I can't wait to tackle the next challenge.
